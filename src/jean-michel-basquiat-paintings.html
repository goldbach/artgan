Jean-Michel Basquiat Paintings

Jean-Michel Basquiat (December 22, 1960 – August 12, 1988) was an American artist. In the late 1970s, he started as a rather inconspicuous graffiti artist in New York City and by the 1980s became a Neo-expressionist and Primitivist painter. Learn more »
